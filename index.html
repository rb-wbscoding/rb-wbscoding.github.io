Norway

Norway a land up north somewhere straddling the polar circle. It is full of Norwegians who like fish (but not chips). They struck lucky with oil and as they are sensible chose not to blow it all, like other countries. This makes the country quite prosperous, i.e. unlike almost all countries in the world it is not in debt.

The mountains

Yes the country for visitors is a bit on the expensive side, but look it is dam pretty. You can see plenty of sharp sided mountains, deep lakes some glaciers and even a moose or two.
